About the Franchise

A fan page dedicated to "Avengers: Endgame" (2019). Explore the exciting world of "Avengers: Endgame" with character bios, trailers, quotes, and a fun trivia quiz!

Character Bios

Iron Man (Tony Stark)

Description: Genius billionaire playboy who became the armored hero. The man who kickstarted the Avengers with his quick wit and tech brilliance. Fact: Tony created his first suit in a cave and his final suit saved the universe.

Captain America (Steve Rogers)

Description: The embodiment of courage and integrity, Steve Rogers is the super-soldier with a shield that stands for hope and freedom.


Fact: Steve lifted Thor’s hammer in Endgame, proving he’s truly worthy.

Thor

Description: The God of Thunder, wielding the mighty hammer and his lightning powers. From godly royalty to an Avenger with heart and humor.


Fact: Thor lost his hammer in Ragnarok but gained a new weapon, Stormbreaker.

Black Widow (Natasha Romanoff)

Description: A highly-skilled spy with unmatched combat abilities and a heart devoted to her Avengers family.


Fact: Natasha sacrificed herself on Vormir to obtain the Soul Stone, showing her ultimate loyalty.

Hawkeye (Clint Barton)

Description: The master archer with perfect aim, fighting alongside gods and monsters with just a bow and arrow.


Fact: After losing his family in the snap, Clint became a rogue vigilante known as Ronin.

Hulk (Bruce Banner)

Description: A brilliant scientist with an uncontrollable alter-ego. In Endgame, Bruce finally balanced brains and brawn as the "Smart Hulk"


Fact: Bruce used the Infinity Gauntlet to bring back everyone snapped away by Thanos.

Thanos

Description: The Mad Titan, obsessed with balancing the universe by wiping out half of all life. A villain driven by a twisted sense of justice.


Fact: Thanos destroyed the Infinity Stones after his snap, believing his mission was complete.
